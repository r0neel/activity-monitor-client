About

What is this site?

This platform is primarily for coders to anonymously share their experiences with other coders around the world.
We hope to provide a place for coders to just relax and know that they're not alone with their bugs.

What can I share?

You can ask questions, share tips or just talk about something relatable that happened to you or someone you know. Feel free to decorate your posts with a GIF!

Who made this site?

This was a collaborative group project as part of lap 1 of the training course at Futureproof. The project was developed in the span of 1 week from planning to execution to debugging.
